Musical pool

This was made by Nikhil Sonnad, but the concept was inspired by the beautiful clinamen art installation by CÃ©leste Boursier-Mougenot, in which a small symphony is produced by porcelain bowls floating in a pool of water. The piece is now on display at the National Gallery of Victoria, Melbourne.

In this online 'version', white circles move about randomly and emit a sound proportional to their size upon contact. You can drag them around yourself, as well, or change the musical scale used to generate pitch.
